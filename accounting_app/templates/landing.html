PNYN ACCOUNTANTS (PNYN) is a black owned accounting firm offering South African businesses and individual with the financial services solutions. Based in Cape Town. PNYN is equipped with qualified accountants, tax practitioner and business consultants ready and competent to assist our clients. The business was formed in 2023, by the Ngubane family. This was inspired by Mr Maxwell Ngubane the first member of the family who joined the accounting profession. With experience in the accounting industry of more than 25 years both in the public and private sector, with more than 9 years of experience working as a senior finance manager at GJ Crooks Hospital, in Kwazulu-Natal, Scottburgh. This became a family tradition say his son, Phelelani Ngubane and daughter in law, Nothando Ngubane both joined the accounting sector.

We're all about

Accounting solutions!

Monthly Invoicing

Our monthly invoicing service includes the creation and distribution of professional invoices to your clients. We ensure accuracy in billing, track payment statuses, and provide you with a comprehensive overview of your receivables.

Cash Flow Forecasting

Cash flow forecasting involves analyzing your past financial data to predict future cash inflows and outflows. We help you anticipate potential gaps, allowing for proactive decision-making and ensuring your business has the liquidity needed to operate smoothly.

Individual Tax Returns

Our Individual Tax Returns service is tailored for individuals seeking accurate and timely filing of their personal income taxes. We guide you through the process, identify eligible deductions, and maximize your tax benefits while ensuring compliance with tax laws.

VAT Returns:

Value Added Tax (VAT) Returns can be complex, but our service simplifies the process. We handle the preparation and submission of your VAT returns, ensuring accurate reporting of input and output taxes. This helps your business maintain compliance and facilitates smooth interactions with tax authorities.

Payroll Calculation:

Our payroll calculation service covers all aspects of employee compensation. From basic salary calculations to tax deductions and benefits administration, we ensure accurate and compliant payroll processing, saving you time and reducing the risk of errors.

Budget/Forecasts

Our budget and forecasting services involve creating detailed financial plans aligned with your business goals. We work collaboratively with you to set realistic targets, monitor performance against these goals, and make adjustments as needed for optimal financial management.

Statement of Profit/Loss

The Statement of Profit and Loss, also known as the Income Statement, outlines your company's revenues, expenses, and profits over a specific period. We meticulously prepare this statement to help you understand your business's profitability and identify areas for improvement.

Annual Financial Statements

Our Annual Financial Statements service involves the preparation and presentation of comprehensive financial reports summarizing your company's financial performance over the fiscal year. These statements are crucial for stakeholders, providing a clear overview of your business's financial health.

We aim to achieve market growth in this competitive accounting market and also to improve our clients satisfaction by delivering a services that is reliable and reviewed by our Professional Accountants. Our firm also aims to assist new established, small and medium companies with their accounting function to be sustainable at a reasonable rate.
